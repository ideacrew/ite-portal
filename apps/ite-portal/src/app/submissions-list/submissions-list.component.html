Total records = Valid + Failure + Warning

Valid: Count of unique records with NO failure and NO warning in any of the data fields.

Failure: Count of unique records with a failure in one or more data fields. A failure record will not be accepted for further process. and needs to be resubmitted.

Warning: Count of unique records with a warning in one or more data fields but no records have a failure. A warning record should be reviewed and correct information should be resubmitted whenever possible.
